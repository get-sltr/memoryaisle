Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2025

SLTR Digital LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the MemoryAisle mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account, we may collect:

  • Email address
  • Name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Authentication data from third-party providers (Apple, Google, Facebook) if you choose to sign in with these services

1.2 Shopping Data

To provide our services, we collect:

  • Shopping lists and items you create
  • Meal plans and recipes
  • Traditions and preferences
  • Receipt data (if you use receipt scanning)
  • Store locations (if you enable geofencing)

1.3 Usage Data

We automatically collect:

  • Device information (type, operating system, unique device identifiers)
  • App usage statistics
  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Voice commands processed through Mira AI (processed but not stored)

1.4 Location Data

With your permission, we collect location data to:

  • Enable store geofencing features
  • Show your shopping list when you arrive at a store

You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the App
  • Process your shopping lists and sync across devices
  • Enable family sharing features
  • Power Mira AI assistant functionality
  • Send service-related communications
  • Process payments and subscriptions
  • Analyze usage to improve our services
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • OpenAI: AI processing for Mira assistant (queries are processed but not used for training)
  • Stripe: Payment processing
  • Brevo: Email communications

3.2 Family Members

When you share lists with family members, they can see:

  • Shared shopping lists and items
  • Your name and profile photo
  • Activity on shared lists

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Encryption at rest
  • Secure authentication protocols
  • Regular security audits
  • Row-level security for database access

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion:

  • Personal data is deleted within 30 days
  • Backup data is purged within 90 days
  • Anonymized analytics may be retained indefinitely

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and data
  • Portability: Export your data in a machine-readable format
  • Opt-out: Disable certain data collection

To exercise these rights, visit the Delete Account page or contact us at privacy@memoryaisle.app.

7. Children's Privacy

MemoryAisle is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before providing any information.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the new policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last updated" date
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes

11. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell data)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: privacy@memoryaisle.app

Support: support@memoryaisle.app
